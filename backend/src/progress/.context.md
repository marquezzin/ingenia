# App: progress

## Propósito
Acompanhamento do progresso acadêmico do aluno por módulo, aula e exercício.

## Models
- **`StudentModuleProgress`** — Progresso por módulo. Campos: `student_profile` (FK → StudentProfile), `module` (FK → Module), `progress_status`, `started_at` (nullable), `completed_at` (nullable). Unique: `(student_profile, module)`. Indexes: `module`, `progress_status`
- **`StudentLessonProgress`** — Progresso por aula. Campos: `student_profile` (FK → StudentProfile), `lesson` (FK → Lesson), `progress_status`, `started_at` (nullable), `completed_at` (nullable). Unique: `(student_profile, lesson)`. Indexes: `lesson`, `progress_status`
- **`StudentExerciseProgress`** — Progresso por exercício. Campos: `student_profile` (FK → StudentProfile), `exercise` (FK → Exercise), `progress_status`, `attempts_count` (default=0), `first_attempt_at` (nullable), `completed_at` (nullable). Unique: `(student_profile, exercise)`. Indexes: `exercise`, `progress_status`

## Enums (`enums.py`)
- **`ProgressStatus`**: NOT_STARTED, IN_PROGRESS, COMPLETED

## Invariantes (via `clean()`)
- `completed_at` preenchido → `progress_status = COMPLETED`
- `progress_status = NOT_STARTED` → `started_at` e `completed_at` devem ser nulos
- `attempts_count >= 0` (via `PositiveIntegerField`)
